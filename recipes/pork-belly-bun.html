Pork Belly Buns

Description

A great starter to a dinner party.

Ingrediants

  • 2 ounces of Pork belly
  • Pickled Red Onions
  • Sliced Cucumber
  • 0.5 Ounce of Spicy Aioli
  • 2 Steamed Rice Buns

Steps

  1. Grill pork belly.
  2. While the pork belly is grilling, steam the rice buns.
  3. Slice cucumber.
  4. Assemble all ingrediants into sandwich.

Short Rib Empanadas

Description

This is the recipe for Sushi Hatsu's famous Short Rib Empanadas. These empanadas use ingrediants found in Asian cuisine, and are delivered through this classic South American dish.

Ingrediants

  • 2 ounces of Korean Braised Short Rib
  • 2000 grams of AP Flour
  • 480 grams of Butter
  • 800 grams of water
  • 240 grams of sugar
  • 13 grams of salt

Steps

  1. Blitz butter and flour together in a food processor.
  2. Combine water, sugar, and salt to dissolve.
  3. Mix all ingrediants and knead into ball.
  4. Plastic wrap the dough and rest for one hour in the fridge.
  5. Make a small cirle of dough and add a small ball of short rib.
  6. Use an empanada presser to fill dough.
  7. Deep fry until golden brown.
  8. Serve with Shiso Chimichurri

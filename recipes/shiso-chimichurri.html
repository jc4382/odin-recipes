Shiso Chimichurri Sauce

Description

A South American classic with an Asian twist.

Ingrediants

  • 4 Bunches of Shiso Leaves
  • 2 Bunches of Parsley
  • 3 Shallots
  • 10 Cloves of Garlic
  • 6 Ounces of Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 16 Ounces of Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Finely chop parsley and shiso leaves.
  2. Fine dice shallots.
  3. Grate garlic.
  4. Add all other ingrediants together and whisk.
  5. Add salt to taste.
  6. Serve with Short Rib Empanadas
